📄 About

JDGroupAccessBot is a Telegram bot that checks if users are subscribed to your YouTube channel before letting them into your private Telegram group. It's fast, secure, and makes community access simple and fair.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What does the bot do?

It verifies if someone is subscribed to your YouTube channel before allowing them access to a private Telegram group.

Is it safe?

Yes. It uses secure Google OAuth and only reads subscription data. We never store or share sensitive information.

How do I start?

Just click the Start button and follow the verification process. Simple and smooth.

🔐 Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. JDGroupAccessBot only accesses your public YouTube subscription data during verification. Your Telegram and YouTube information is never stored, shared, or sold. All invite links are one-time use and expire once claimed.
