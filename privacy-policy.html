Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how JDGroupAccessBot ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our YouTube subscription verification services to access private Telegram groups.

1. Information We Collect

  • Google Account Information: Name, email address, and public subscription status to a specific YouTube channel.
  • Telegram User ID: Collected to send the invite link upon successful verification.

2. How We Use the Information

  • To verify that a user is subscribed to a designated YouTube channel.
  • To generate and deliver a one-time private Telegram invite link.
  • To ensure users are legitimate subscribers before granting group access.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored securely and encrypted using modern security practices.
  • User data is retained only as long as necessary to complete the verification process.
  • We implement strict access controls and do not store unnecessary personal data.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.
  • Data is used solely for internal operations and verification purposes.

5. Use of Google APIs

JDGroupAccessBot’s use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

6. User Rights

  • Users may request deletion of their data by contacting us via the support form.
  • Users may revoke access via their Google Account’s security settings.

7. Children's Privacy

This service is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Effective Date.” Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us via the form provided in the app or email us at support@example.com.
